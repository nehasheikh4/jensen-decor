JANSEN DECOR

Jackson Design specializes in the best of Scandinavian and international vintage design 1900 - 2000 with main focus on Scandinavian classics. Established in 1981, Jackson Design have built one of the most extensive collections of 20th century Scandinavian and International vintage design, consistently offering rare and museum quality examples. In addition to private collectors, our pieces are located within the collections of major museums and public institutions internationally.Jackson Design regularly participates in international design fairs, such as Design Miami/Basel, Modernism New York and TEFAF Maastricht. Paul Jackson has been part of the vetting team at both TEFAF Maastricht and Design Miami/Basel. Our website is updated regularly, however if you are searching for something in particular, please do not hesitate to contact us
